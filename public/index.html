How to prevent Corona Virus?

Getting vaccinated and boosted is the best way to reduce your risk of symptoms, especially becoming severely ill, if you get COVID-19. But right along with vaccination are steps you can take to both avoid getting infected and help prevent spreading the virus to others.

Symptoms of Corona Virus

Detailed of Symptoms of Corona Virus

High Fever

High temperature (fever) is a common symptom of coronavirus (covid-19) infection

Cough

COVID-19 is a respiratory viral infection that causes symptoms such as fatigue, fever, and a dry cough.

Sore Throat

A sore throat is a shared symptom of many illnesses, including COVID-19.

Headache

Headache usually begins early in the symptomatic phase of COVID-19
